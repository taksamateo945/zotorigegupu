Jersey Shore's Ronnie Ortiz-Magro Holds JWoww's Baby Meilani: Picture

Publish date: 2024-04-01

Uncle Ronnie loves Meilani! Jenni "JWoww" Farley's former Jersey Shore costar Ronnie Ortiz-Magro shared a sweet moment with her newborn baby daughter, Meilani Alexandra Mathews, on Tuesday, Aug. 19, the same day he revealed his split from Sammi "Sweetheart" Giancola.

Dressed in a pink-checkered shirt, a yellow tie, and a charcoal suit, Magro stopped by JWoww's home to spend time with her 5-week-old little girl prior to the New York premiere of Jersey Shore Massacre. He shared a photo of himself cradling Meilani via Instagram, writing, "#Family #LoveThem @rogermathewsnj #Heading to the @jerseyshoremassacre to support @jennijwowwmtv #RealMenWearPink #Meilani"

Farley's fiance, Roger Mathews, retweeted the photo on Aug. 20, adding his own funny caption. "Hi uNkle RoNnie," it read. "I'm still cuter than you even with my poop diaper. Smell better too."

As previously reported, Magro, 28, split from longtime love Giancola, 27, about two months ago. Speaking with Us Weekly at the Jersey Shore Massacre premiere, he explained that they "grew [their] separate ways" but are still close.
