Kylo Ren goes undercover for Undercover Boss

Publish date: 2024-06-03

Adam Driver, host of this week’s SNL, got back into his famous character, Kylo Ren, from Star Wars: The Force Awakens. Kylo did an “Undercover Boss” spoof segment and spoke to his employees and stormtroopers at Starkiller Base. When he asked what they thought of him, things didn’t go as he expected resulting him to totally flip out. He disguised himself as a radar technician named Matt, but his raging personality wasn’t disguised whatsoever.
