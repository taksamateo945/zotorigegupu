Who Is Trevor Noah Dating? Actress Minka Kelly Is His Girlfriend!

Publish date: 2024-03-23

Trevor Noah is hosting the 2021 Grammy Awards later this evening, but right now, we’re highlighting his girlfriend Minka Kelly!

You may not have even known that the 37-year-old Daily Show host is dating the 40-year-old Friday Night Lights actress because they have kept their relationship so low key.

They were first linked back in August of 2020 when a source told People that the two stars are in a relationship.

“Minka‘s never been happier,” the insider shared with the site. “It’s a very serious relationship.”

There was a big clue in November of 2020 about just how serious the couple appeared to be when they were seen looking for houses together.

This year, we didn’t have much news about Minka and Trevor at first. However, a source eventually spoke out and revealed if they’re still together (they are – and this update is so sweet!)

It’s unclear if Minka will be joining Trevor at the Grammys tonight, but they have rarely been photographed together.

Be sure to check out the 2021 Grammys tonight on CBS.
