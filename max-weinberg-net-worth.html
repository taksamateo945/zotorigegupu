Early Life and Career

Weinberg’s passion for music ignited at a young age when he discovered his love for the drums. In 1974, a significant milestone unfolded as he joined Bruce Springsteen’s E Street Band, propelling him to new heights and showcasing his ability to contribute to the success of a legendary group. Weinberg’s drumming skills have consistently elevated the performances of iconic figures such as James Brown, Paul McCartney, Bob Dylan, BB King, Tony Bennett, and Ringo Starr.
