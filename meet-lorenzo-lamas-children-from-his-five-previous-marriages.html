How Many Children Does Lorenzo Lamas Have From His Previous Marriages?

Lorenzo Lamas has six children in all, but he had five of them – a son and five daughters – from his wives, including Alvaro Joshua “AJ,” Shayne, Alexandra Lynne, Victoria Arlene, and Isabella Lorenza.

The name of his daughter with the Star Trek: Deep Space Nine actress is Paton Lee. With that, we look at the lives of the six known children born to the Renegade actor Lorenzo Lamas.

Lorenzo Lamas Was Legally Married To Five Women At Different Times

The American actor has had a very complex love story involving different women he once thought he loved but fell out of love with. Lorenzo Lamas can attest to the ups and downs of marriage as he has been in five different marital unions.

A Brief Look At All Lorenzo Lamas’ Marriages

1. Lorenzo Lamas and Victoria Hilbert: 1981 – 1982

Lorenzo Lamas’ first attempt at marriage was to his first love, a woman named Victoria Hilbert. It was short as the marriage, which was instituted in 1981, ended just one year later in 1982. It is no surprise that the pair did not have any children together, obviously due to the brief duration of the marriage.

2. Lorenzo Lamas and Michele Cathy Smith: 1983 – 1985

Lorenzo went on to have a relationship with Michele Cathy Smith, his personal publicist. The lovers would then have two children together after getting married in 1983. The names of the two children Michele and Lorenzo Lamas had together are Alvaro Joshua “AJ” (son) and Shayne Lamas (daughter).

Lorenzo and Michele’s marriage lasted longer than his first, as the pair were married for two years. They eventually divorced in 1985 but had a protected battle for custody of the children, who were both babies. The custody fight lingered for several years.

3. Lorenzo Lamas and Kathleen Kinmont: 1989 – 1993

It did not take time for Lorenzo to get into the relationship pool again; this time, with a woman named Kathleen Kinmont, who is said to be the daughter of actress Gladys Marlene Wasde, popularly known as Abby Dalton. They tied the nuptial knots in 1989. However, it has been said that they had been together for a long time before eventually heading to the altar.

Their marriage lasted even longer than Lorenzo’s first two marriages, as the pair were together for four years before eventually getting divorced in 1993. However, the couple did not have children together.

4. Lorenzo Lamas and Shauna Sand: 1996 – 2002

Three years after Lorenzo’s third marriage, in 1996, he remarried, and this time, he married Shauna Sand. Lorenzo and Shauna Sand had three children together, and they were all beautiful girls – Alexandra Lynne, Victoria Lamas, and Isabella Lorenza.

The union of Lorenzo and Shauna Sand lasted for six years. Unfortunately, the pair eventually filed for a divorce in 2002, and Lorenzo got a restraining order.

5. Lorenzo Lamas and Shawna Craig: 2011 – 2018

Lorenzo Lamas tried marriage again as he was engaged to Playboy playmate Barbara Moore in 2004. A wedding was planned to take place in 2005, but it was unfortunately called off by Lorenzo Lamas a night before the D-day. It was later revealed that the decision to cancel the wedding was because Barbara had given a male stripper fellatio at her bachelorette party.

The following year, Lamas was in the dating pool again, and this time he started courting Second Wives Club star, Shawna Craig. And five years later, they headed to the altar. The wedding took place on April 30, 2011, in Mexico, and the marriage lasted for seven years but produced no children.

However, as is always the case, another Lorenzo Lamas marriage headed for the rocks and ended in June 2018. The cause of divorce was alleged infidelity on the part of Shawna Craig.
