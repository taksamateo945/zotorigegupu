Sundance Head added to Blake Shelton's tour

Publish date: 2024-02-15

Blake Shelton announced today that Sundance Head will join him on his Doing It To Country Songs tour this year.

Head won season eleven of The Voice US last year as part of #TeamBlake. His winning song “Darlin’ Don’t Go” reached number four on the US Country chart.

Head said of the opportunity, “I’m just excited about being able to perform with a great entertainer and learn something from him while I’m on the road. It’s going to be the most fun thing I’ve ever done.”

Watch his winning song below:

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=l9rKJHOacYQ

Rae Lynn, who was a contestant on season two of The Voice US in 2015, will also join the tour. She is due to release her debut album Wild Horse later this year.

Shelton said of the tour, “I’m excited to get back out on the road and bring new music, some previous hits and a few surprises. Having Rae Lynn on the tour again is terrific, and I’m thrilled Sundance is joining and bringing his incredible musical talents.”

The thirteen date tour kicks off in Bakersfield, California on 16 February. It will also visit Las Vegas, Nevada and Sacremento, California before finishing on 18 March in Omaha, Nebraska.
