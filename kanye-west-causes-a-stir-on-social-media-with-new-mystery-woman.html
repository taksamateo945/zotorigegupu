Jason Kelce jokes brother Travis 'looks like he's auditioning for a new Wes Anderson film' as video

Publish date: 2024-08-24

Travis Kelce might be getting into game mode tonight but that hasn’t stopped Jason Kelce from having his say on his dress sense on social media.

Travis and his Chiefs team touched down in Buffalo on Saturday afternoon and play the Bills at Highmark Stadium on Sunday in a hotly anticipated playoff game.

The Chiefs filmed Kelce leaving the plane in a mustard yellow suit, striped turtleneck, beanie hat and shades and as soon as Jason saw the footage, he had his say.

‘Looks like he’s auditioning for a new Wes Anderson film,’ he joked about his brother.

Travis, who at 34 is two years younger than Jason, has been linked with a foray into the acting world after his playing days are over.

Travis Kelce was filmed on arrival in Buffalo by the Chiefs’ social media team on Saturday 

Exclusive DailyMail.Com pictures show Kelce arriving in Buffalo on Saturday afternoon

Kelce and his Chiefs team have to beat the Bills on Sunday – or their season is over 

His brother, Jason, quickly took to X to let everyone know what he thought of his outfit 

The younger Kelce has also hosted Saturday Night Live before and has also previously been claimed to be interested in pursuing that avenue in his life after football.

But for now, the game is very much center stage and brother Jason and his wife, Kylie, are set to fly to Buffalo on Sunday to cheer Travis and the Chiefs on.

It could well be the first time Jason has met Travis’s girlfriend Taylor Swift – the singer is also expected to make the trip from Manhattan on Sunday.

Jason had hinted on a couple occasions this week that he was considering making the trip to cheer his younger brother on.

Jason, of course, has a free weekend after the Eagles went out of the playoffs at the hands of the Tampa Bay Buccaneers on Monday. It has plunged Jason’s long-term football future into doubt, with reports on Tuesday morning emerging that he told the Eagles locker room that he is retiring.

But since then, the 36-year-old has stayed coy over what the future holds and Travis even suggested on Friday that his big brother could play on. 

No timeline has been given as to when Kelce expects to announce his football future. 

But if he too would like a move into acting, he might start by following his brother’s fashion sense.
