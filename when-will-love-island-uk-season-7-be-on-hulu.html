When Will Love Island UK Season 7 Be On Hulu?

Publish date: 2024-04-07

Summer 2021 just got a little bit hotter!

Love Island UK has finally returned for another round of addicting reality mayhem. After a 16-month absence, Season 7 of the immensely popular series premiered June 28, 2021 on ITV2 and ITV Hub in the UK. Hosted by Laura Whitmore, 11 Islanders have entered the villa looking to, in some order, make a connection, find love, and get a killer tan.

Last season, fans in the U.S. were able to watch the series on Hulu, with the streamer adding five episodes each Saturday (usually about two-three weeks after they aired in the UK). Will the series follow a similar schedule for Season 7? We can help you out with that question.

When exactly will Love Island UK Season 7 arrive on Hulu? Here’s everything we know.

IS LOVE ISLAND UK ON HULU?

You bet! If you’re looking to beat that summer heat, Seasons 1-6 of Love Island UK are currently streaming on Hulu.

WILL LOVE ISLAND UK SEASON 7 BE ON HULU?

Yes! Earlier this month, it was announced that Love Island UK Season 7 would also be available to stream on Hulu! More information below.

WHEN WILL LOVE ISLAND UK SEASON 7 BE ON HULU?

Hulu’s “new on July” revealed that the Love Island UK Season 7 premiere debuts Monday, July 12, 2021 on Hulu. As of now, we don’t have any official information about the show’s release schedule. For Season 6, Hulu would add five episodes each Saturday (usually about two-three weeks after they aired in the UK), but the plans for the new season are currently unknown. Decider has reached out to Hulu for clarification, and we’ll update this article with any new information.
