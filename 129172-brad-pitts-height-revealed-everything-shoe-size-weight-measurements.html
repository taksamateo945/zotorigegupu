Who is Bradley Pitt?

Bradley Pitt is an American actor and film producer. He was born on 18th December 1963 to William Alvin Pitt and Jane Etta in Shawnee, Oklahoma. His father was the proprietor of a trucking company, while his mother worked as a school counsellor. Shortly after his birth, his parents relocated to Springfield, Missouri, together with his siblings: Douglas Mitchell and Julie Neal.
