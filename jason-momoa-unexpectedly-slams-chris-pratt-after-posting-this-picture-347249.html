Jason Momoa Unexpectedly Slams Chris Pratt After Posting THIS Picture!

Publish date: 2024-06-09

Jason Momoa had a pretty unexpected reason to call out fellow actor Chris Pratt on social media and he held nothing back! The star did his duty as ‘Aquaman’ and pointed out that Chris should try harder to protect the oceans by giving up the one-time-use plastic and instead, going for a reusable water bottle!

It all started with Pratt sharing a pic that featured himself with such a single-use bottle while at the gym.

The snap was supposed to be a promotion for some new products that he uses while working out.

Soon after, he actor’s buff arms attracted a lot of praise from friends, family members, and, of course, fans!

For instance, his brother in law, Patrick Schwarzenegger, gushed over him with a comment that reads: ‘Lookin ripped!’

His wife, Katherine, also went ahead and showed him love by leaving a heart eyes emoji under the post.

Sean Hayes jokingly commented: ‘Is that a picture of me? Oh, wait. No. No it is not me. Common mistake.’

But Momoa was not as impressed!

‘Bro i love u but wtf on the water bottle. no single use plastic. come on,’ he called the other out.
