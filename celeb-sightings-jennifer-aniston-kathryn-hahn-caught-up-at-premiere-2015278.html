Celeb Sightings: Jennifer Aniston, Kathryn Hahn Caught Up at Premiere

Publish date: 2024-08-31

—Jennifer Aniston and Kathryn Hahn attended the She’s Funny That Way premiere afterparty at the Sunset Tower hotel in West Hollywood, where guests sipped on Jose Cuervo margaritas.

—Viola Davis celebrated her 50th birthday with her How to Get Away with Murder on-screen husband, Tom Verica, at Hutchinson Cocktails & Grill in L.A.

—Carmelo Anthony and chef Michael White co-hosted an exclusive dinner to celebrate Altamarea Group’s newest New York hot spot, Vaucluse, with the Wine N Dine app.

—Lenny Kravitz and Gary Clark Jr. rocked out at Brooklyn’s Afropunk Festival.

—Russell Simmons talked to Leonardo DiCaprio about producing a new show about Kosher Soul stars O’Neal McKnight and Miriam Sternoff at a house party in Malibu, Calif.

–New York City Mayor Bill de Blasio enjoyed a lunch of Bronx Pale Ales and lobster salads with a colleague at The Lure Group’s Ambrose Beer and Lobster in NYC. 

–Male model Marcus Schenkenberg took a workout class via www.bodyartmedia.com at his home in Miami.

—Andi Dorfman was styled by Matrix SoColor celebrity stylist Nick Stenson with Matrix StyleLink products in NYC.

—Billy Ray Cyrus, with producer Jason Charles Miller and actor Maxwell Glick, attended the Muzzled the Musical screening, sponsored by Vital Farms, Fresh Brothers Pizza, and LootCrate, at Dragonfly in Hollywood.

—Maria Sharapova celebrated her Sugarpova pop-up store at the Bloomingdale’s 59th Street flagship store in NYC.

—Kaitlyn Bristowe and Shawn Booth shared cocktails and small bites at STK LA.

—Perrie Edwards wore a Missguided Metallic Side Split Knitted Maxi Dress in silver during Billboard’s Hot 100 Festival, held at Jones Beach in N.Y.

—Vanessa Hudgens wore a sweatshirt from Brokedown Clothing in L.A.
