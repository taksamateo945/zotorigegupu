Jennifer Love Hewitt Wants a Can't Hardly Wait Sequel

Publish date: 2024-07-10

Give the girl what she wants! Jennifer Love Hewitt went to a Cinespia screening of her ’90s movie Can’t Hardly Wait at the Hollywood Forever Cemetery in L.A. this past weekend, and has been tweeting about a possible reunion flick.

Love Hewitt — whose film credits include hits such as I Know What You Did Last Summer and Heartbreakers — wrote on Sunday, Aug. 16, that she was “proud” to have starred in the teen comedy.

From @cinespia Hollywood Forever Cemetery Screening last night. pic.twitter.com/2M00L3Dig9

— Jennifer Love Hewitt (@TheReal_Jlh) August 16, 2015

So proud to have been a part of this movie! #CantHardlyWait @cinespia pic.twitter.com/udRfubUERo

— Jennifer Love Hewitt (@TheReal_Jlh) August 16, 2015

In the film, Love Hewitt played “dream girl” Amanda Beckett, who was embroiled in a senior-year high school love triangle with Preston Myers (Embry) and Mike Dexter (Peter Facinelli). Seth Green, Lauren Ambrose, and Jaime Pressly also costarred in the flick.

The actress concluded her updates about the movie on Sunday, pleading with her 935,000-plus followers on Twitter to make a sequel happen.

Can’t Hardly Wait reunion movie anyone? Let’s get them to make it! pic.twitter.com/rI3XzLbzqD

— Jennifer Love Hewitt (@TheReal_Jlh) August 16, 2015

Her costar Embry, who was also at the screening on Saturday, chimed in via Twitter, too.
