Holly Hagan is a British television personality, best known for appearing in MTV’s raucous reality show Geordie Shore.

Geordie Shore, the British version of the American show Jersey Shore, hit television screens back in 2011 and saw Holly and her co-stars Charlotte Crosby, Gaz Beadle, Sophie Kasaei, Vicky Pattinson, Jay Gardner, James Tindale and Greg Lake, cause carnage on the streets of Newcastle.

Hagan had a pretty tumultuous time on the show and despite originally struggling to fit in with her housemates, she soon formed strong bonds with Crosby and Kasaei.

Holly appeared on the show for 13 seasons before leaving in 2016.
